MINI GAMES

Mini games are so much more different to normal Minecraft play.

BATTLE

Firstly, let's talk about the 'Battle' mini game which most people find frustrating. One reason is that they think the chests are hard to find and secondly, if they don't know where the chests are, they try to hoard the starter chests but then they're killed. The trick is to get at least one chest from the middle but then explore the outer arena. If you know where a chest is but it is very likely someone will try to get it, go to that place first then go to the middle (once the chests have sparked).
